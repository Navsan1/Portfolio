About Me

I'm Pranav Verma, a passionate Computer Science graduate from Wilfrid Laurier University with a knack for blending creativity and technology. My journey in tech has been driven by a deep interest in building innovative solutions, from crafting user-friendly websites to developing immersive AR applications. Projects like creating a course-rating platform at Wlugo.com and leading an inventory management system honed my skills in front-end development and database integration using tools like Java, Python, Node.js, and SQL. I’ve also explored game development with a C++ Tetris game and ventured into AI with a Discord bot, reflecting my love for diverse challenges.

Beyond coding, I’m fascinated by augmented reality and 3D modeling, as seen in my work on a Foodland Ontario AR app using JavaScript and Blender. Leading the Artificial Intelligence Club as Founding President fueled my enthusiasm for community-building and sharing knowledge. My co-op experiences, including a role at the Ministry of Northern Development & Mines, taught me to bridge strategy and execution through tools like Power BI and PowerApps.
